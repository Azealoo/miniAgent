# Guide Risk Checklist

## Goal

Use this checklist before committing to target selection or guide design interpretation.

## Common risk categories

- Essentiality or strong fitness effects
- Isoform complexity
- Gene family redundancy
- Context-dependent biology
- Expected toxicity or proliferation defects
- Weak prior evidence in the chosen system

## Practical questions

- Is the target likely essential in this cell type?
- Could a weak phenotype reflect compensation rather than biology absence?
- Are there multiple isoforms that complicate interpretation?
- Is the expected phenotype too indirect for the planned readout?
- Are there known stress or viability artifacts?

## Suggested outputs

- Risk category
- Why it matters
- Confidence
- Suggested mitigation
